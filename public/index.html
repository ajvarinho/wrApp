A small app loving the bubble wrap and modernist art of former Yugoslavia.

The UI is a tribute to the original poster for second Nove Tendencije ("New Tendencies") exhibition in Zagreb, 1963. It was designed by artist Ivan Picelj, and you can see the original here.

New Tendencies

Five exhibitions organized between 1961-1973 in the Gallery of Modern Art in Zagreb.
Based on EXAT 51's experiments in post-constructivist art, the works on exhibitions explored the relation between visual arts and contemporary technology, introducing computer-generated graphics and a way to present and distribute art beyond the limitations of traditional art markets and institutions.
